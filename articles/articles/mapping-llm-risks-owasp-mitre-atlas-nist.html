Mapping LLM Risks to OWASP, MITRE ATLAS, and NIST AI RMF

By James Fiyci | Updated July 10, 2026

Security notes are stronger when they are clear. A useful AI security project should explain not only what was built, but how the risk maps to frameworks that teams already recognize.

OWASP LLM Top 10

OWASP is useful for application-level risks such as prompt injection, sensitive information disclosure, supply chain issues, insecure output handling, and excessive agency.

MITRE ATLAS

MITRE ATLAS is useful when describing adversary behavior against AI systems. It gives defenders a common vocabulary for AI-focused tactics and techniques.

NIST AI RMF

NIST AI RMF is useful for governance language. It helps teams explain how they govern, map, measure, and manage AI risk across an application lifecycle.
