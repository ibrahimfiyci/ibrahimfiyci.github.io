AI Bill of Materials and Model Supply Chain Security

By James Fiyci | Updated July 10, 2026

AI applications depend on more than code. They rely on models, datasets, prompts, embeddings, APIs, containers, plugins, and deployment settings. A useful AI inventory should make those parts visible.

What to inventory

At minimum, teams should know model name, version, provider, training or source context where available, runtime dependencies, dataset references, licenses, API integrations, and owners.

Why this matters

AI supply-chain work connects cybersecurity, governance, engineering, and risk communication. It is a strong signal that a candidate understands production security, not only prompts.
