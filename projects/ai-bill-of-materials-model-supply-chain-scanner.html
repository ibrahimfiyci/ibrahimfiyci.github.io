Technical Project

AI Bill of Materials & Model Supply Chain Risk Scanner

A prototype for documenting AI components, model provenance, dependency context, and supply-chain risk in machine-readable form.

Project Overview

The scanner inventories model metadata, runtime dependencies, datasets, licenses, container images, and known vulnerability context. The goal is to make AI systems easier to review, update, and govern before they enter production.

Technologies

  • Python
  • CycloneDX and SPDX concepts
  • Model cards
  • Container metadata
  • Supply-chain risk scoring

Security focus

AI systems depend on models, datasets, packages, APIs, prompts, and deployment infrastructure. This project treats that inventory as a security asset, similar to how SBOMs help teams understand software supply-chain exposure.

Output

The scanner produces a concise component summary, missing metadata findings, license notes, dependency risk flags, and follow-up recommendations for owners of AI-enabled applications.
