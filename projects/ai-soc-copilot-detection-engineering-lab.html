Technical Project

AI SOC Copilot Evaluation & Detection Engineering Lab

A lab for testing whether AI-assisted SOC workflows improve analyst speed without losing evidence quality, accuracy, or reviewability.

Project Overview

This lab evaluates AI triage summaries against known alert evidence, requiring citations, confidence notes, and analyst review. It also explores how AI can assist with detection engineering without inventing unsupported conclusions.

Technologies

  • Python
  • Sigma-style detections
  • SIEM alert samples
  • LLM evaluation rubrics
  • Analyst-in-the-loop review

Evaluation questions

The lab asks whether an AI assistant can summarize an alert, cite evidence, identify missing context, propose next triage steps, and avoid unsupported claims. The goal is augmentation, not blind automation.

Detection engineering

A controlled workflow compares AI-generated detection ideas against expected telemetry, false-positive risk, ATT&CK-style behavior, and analyst review notes before anything is considered usable.

Study notes

This project demonstrates SOC awareness, detection engineering vocabulary, AI evaluation discipline, and practical thinking about human review in security operations.
